A simple, reflective tool to explore how team roles interact with different leadership contexts.

How to Use This Tool
  1. Select a scenario based on the situation you're leading.
  2. Choose three roles you believe would work best in that context.
  3. Hover over each role to understand what it contributes.
  4. Review the outcome to see how well the team fits the scenario.
  5. Experiment again with different combinations to deepen your understanding.

This is not a test. There are no perfect teams ‚Äî only better fits for specific moments.

About the Roles: These five roles are inspired by Meredith Belbin's Team Roles framework, developed in the 1970s and still widely used today. Belbin's work focuses on how people contribute to teams, not their titles or seniority. I intentionally didn't include all nine roles to keep the demo focused and lightweight. Reflection, not assessment, is the goal.
